Around the World

Not all countries responded to the pandemic in the same way. In Around the World, we look at patterns of systematic, pandemic leadership in a way that is out-of-the- box and causes you to consider the pandemic in light of the latest news. This conference is for anyone seeking to learn more about the global context of the pandemic: employers, students, and even researchers. We are organizing this conference in the hope that it will illuminate your perspective of the pandemic on a global scale.

About Meenta, Inc.

Meenta Inc. (learn more by clicking here) is a biotech that seeks to make all of the scientific equipment and services in the world accessible. They accomplish that by creating digital platforms that connect science and improve our use of the amazing technology available to us. However, they realize that all of the scientific equipment in the world is useless without the ability to interpret it.

About DemocratizingScience

DemocratizingScience is a grassroots initiative, created in the spirit of Meenta, Inc.'s overall mission to make science more accessible. DemocratizingScience brings the spirit of Meenta, Inc. to local communities around the globe via events like this.
DemocratizingScience events are organized by passionate individuals who seek to uncover new ideas and the latest research related to COVID-19. DemocratizingScience events include live speakers and recorded talks, and are organized independently under a free license granted by Meenta Inc.

About Around the World

At Around the World, we want to inspire deeper thought about the responses of leadership to the pandemic around the world. We are aspiring to bring light to inequity, and give passionate people the room to use their expertise to give us a fresh perspective regarding what's happening outside of quarantine. In a world where fear and division seem to rule, Around the World is looking to help us understand our own context better.
